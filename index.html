About Ratanji Farm House

Nestled in the heart of Jardi Mohammadpur, Bihar, Ratanji Farm House offers an authentic farmhouse experience with modern comforts. Surrounded by verdant fields and fresh air, it's the perfect escape from city hustle.

Our property features spacious accommodations, organic farm-fresh meals, and activities like nature walks, bird watching, and farm tours.
